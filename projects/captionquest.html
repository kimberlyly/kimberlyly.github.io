CaptionQuest Creativity Challenges!

A cute, silly game that connects people together with funny captions of every day scenes.

For our COGS 120 class, our focus was gaming. We wanted a game that will brighten people's day and to take notice of the world around them. So, we came up with Caption Quest. Users will take pictures of whatever they please, and people can caption the image. The caption that gets the most likes will be rewarded points, which they can use on cute pets!

Project Details

  • I was the front-end developer on this project.
  • We worked with node.js and mongodb to keep track of our users and their data.
  • I was involved with the user interviews, wireframing, and prototyping.
